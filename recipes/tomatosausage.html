Tomato Sausage Pasta

Description

Pasta with marinara sauce, spicy sausages, and a bit of a kick.

Let's get started!

Ingredients

Serves 2

  • 1 can of pureed tomatoes
  • Half an onion, chopped
  • 6 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 250g dried pasta of any kind
  • 6 spicy sausages, chopped into circular pieces
  • Dried oregano
  • Fresh basil
  • Olive oil
  • Chilli flakes
  • Ricotta to serve

Steps

  1. To make the marinara sauce, first combine pureed tomatoes, onion, garlic, dried oregano, chilli flakes, and olive oil in a pot.
  2. Bring the sauce to a simmer, then lower the heat and allow it to continue simmering for about 45 minutes, until droplets of oil are visible on the surface of the sauce.
  3. Add salt and/or pepper to taste, then set the sauce aside.
  4. In a pan, fry the sausage pieces until they are brown on both sides. For extra flavor, do not discard the oil that is left in the pan after frying the sausages.
  5. In a separate pan, cook the pasta until al dente in salted water according to packet instructions.
  6. Drain the pasta, place it back in a pan, and mix in the marinara sauce, sausages, and oil resserved from the fried sausages.
  7. Top the pasta with a few spoonfuls of ricotta and garnish with torn basil leaves before serving. Add more chilli flakes to taste.
