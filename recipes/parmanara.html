Parma-nara

Description

While carbonara is usually made with bacon, this recipe uses parma ham as a healthier - and equally delicious - alternative.

Let's get started!

Ingredients

Serves 2

  • 5 slices of parma ham, cut into strips
  • 250g dried pasta of any kind
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 4 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 40g grated parmesan
  • Chopped fresh parsley OR dried parsley

Steps

  1. Fry the strips of parma ham in a frying pan, without adding oil, until they turn crisp. Set them aside.
  2. Combine the eggs, egg yolk, garlic, and parmesan in a bowl and mix, then set the mixture aside.
  3. In a pan, cook the pasta until al dente in salted water according to packet instructions.
  4. Drain away most of the pasta water, leaving only about one ladle-full of pasta water in the pan.
  5. Add the egg mixture into the pan with the pasta, and stir until the pasta is coated in the sauce, for about 2 minutes.
  6. Add in the strips of parma ham and mix.
  7. Serve with ground black pepper, parsley, and more cheese.
